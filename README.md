## Interview Preparation Tips
- Practice **300+ DSA problems** on platforms like LeetCode and GeeksforGeeks.  
- Strengthen your **Java or MERN stack knowledge** depending on your target role.  
- Use **mock interviews** (Pramp, InterviewBit) to build confidence.  
- Study recent interview experiences shared on sites like Glassdoor or TeamBlind.  
- Build and maintain **real-world projects** to showcase practical skills.  
- Stay consistent and revise fundamentals regularly.
